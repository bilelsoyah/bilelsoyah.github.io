Penguin Groove Festival

Amiens, a city where nature and culture intertwine, offers a one-of-a-kind experience.

Through this festival, we aim to highlight its unique charm—from the serene ambiance

of the Zoo Amiens Métropole to the lively beats of world-class electronic artists,

and the inspiring mission of wildlife conservation. This event celebrates the fusion of

music, art, and nature, creating unforgettable memories while supporting a meaningful cause.
