Live Model Drawing Project

In this project, I explored the vibrant possibilities of pastels to capture the human form with energy and texture.

Breaking traditional norms, I embraced distortions—stretching, twisting, and compressing figures—

to create playful, exaggerated depictions that remain strangely relatable.

This approach brought a dynamic and humorous edge to each piece, redefining the portrayal of the human body.
