Graphic Design Project: "Dialogue"

"Dialogue" is a multidisciplinary graphic design project in collaboration with the talented artist @william.keo.

This box set includes 4 editions (20x26cm), 2 smaller editions, a cartography piece, and a stunning 60x80 poster.

The project explores the fusion of storytelling, design, and visual art, offering a thought-provoking and immersive experience for its audience.
