24H in Barcelona

Barcelona, a city where history meets innovation, offers an endless palette of inspiration.

Through my lens, I aimed to capture its vibrant essence—from the intricate details of Gaudí's

masterpieces to the bustling energy of La Rambla,the quiet charm of hidden alleyways,

and the golden glow of Mediterranean sunsets. This project celebrates Barcelona’s dynamic spirit,

blending its rich cultural heritage with the everyday lives of its people.
